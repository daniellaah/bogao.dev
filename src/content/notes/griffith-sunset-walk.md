Today needed a slower ending.

I had already spent too many hours tightening details on the site, and the usual instinct was to keep going until everything looked “finished.” Walking interrupted that loop.

The useful part was not exercise. It was distance. By the time the light softened, the problems that felt urgent an hour earlier had become much smaller and much easier to name.
