Three quick notes from today:

1. A product page feels calmer when there is one strong visual block instead of three medium ones competing for attention.
2. Most personal tools should probably optimize for return visits, not first-use surprise.
3. Notes that are written too formally often become less true.

I do not want every observation to become an article. Some ideas are better when they stay as fragments.
