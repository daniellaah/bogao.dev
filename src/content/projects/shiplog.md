## What it is

Shiplog is a publishing format for experiments that do not belong in a polished case study yet, but still deserve a durable public record.

## Problem

Model and product timelines are often fragmented across notebooks, commits, screenshots, and temporary notes. That makes it hard to see what was actually tried, what worked, and what failed.

## Approach

Each update is structured around decisions, progress, failed attempts, and next steps, so the output stays readable both as a running log and as future documentation.

## Outcome

The concept is promising because it creates a cleaner bridge between scratchpad experiments and something other people can actually follow.
